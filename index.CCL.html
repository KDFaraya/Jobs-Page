Culinary Arts

Color Coded Labs has a state of the art kitchen that needs your creativity, skills, and passion for culinary arts to bring it to life. We are hiring students who are, or are willing to be, certified food handlers, nutritionists, and chefs to counsel peers in clean eating. We will provide personalized guidance on healthy eating habits, meal prep that can save time and ensure that you have healthy, balanced, and readily available meals 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our program provides compensation, tuition-reduction, and reimbursements for the necessary trainings. Join us, together we can support our overall health and well-being.

Custodian

Custodial work plays a vital role in maintaining the cleanliness and overall appearance of our campus. With the rising cost of tuition, CCL is always looking for ways to reduce expenses and offer affordable education to our students. One solution is to hire student staff to maintain the campus instead of outsourcing the work to third-party cleaning companies. We would like to save those fees and redirect the funds towards lowering the tuition. This would entail our staff to maintain hallways, bathrooms, and common areas. We want to make sure to protect our students by keeping our surfaces disinfected and sustain a healthy environment. CCL has amazing natural lighting and a wonderful view that student staff keep streak-free and shining brightly. Join our team to keep the campus clean!

Tutors

Our students are supported by our highly qualified tutors and teachers. Student Tutors can assist with filling in gaps of the material that weren't fully understood in class. We train all of our tutors to be experts in our CCL courses. We offer tutor led group sessions to encourage peer to peer learning. CCL has beautiful spaces to accommodate both study groups and independent learners. We also offer, but are not limited to, help with homework, one-on-one lesson reviews, and share pro-tips for study skills. Apply for student tutor positions to help your peers while gaining valuable experience and paying off your tuition.
